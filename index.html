THE PAPER ROCK SCISSORS PROJECT

This is a game between you and the computer. Select your weapon by clicking one of the buttons 'Paper', 'Rock' or 'Scissors'. The computer selects its weapon. If your weapon beats computer, you win, otherwise computer wins. If both select the same weapon, then it is a tie.

A Paper wins over Rock(becuase it covers the rock). A Rock wins over scissors(because it breaks scissors). A Scissors wins over paper(because it cuts paper). A Paper loses to Scissors(because it gets cut). A Rock loses to Paper(because it gets covered). A Scissors loses to Rock(because it gets broken)

Chose your weapon by clicking the appropriate button and start the game.
